    Frequently Asked Questions

    Software that allows you to deliver, track, and assess courses and programs is known as a learning management system (LMS).

    • Cloud-Based Solution: The learning content can be accessed from anywhere at any time, regardless of the device, enabling learning at the point of need to track.
    • Progress: Learners and educators can track progress at the course level with various options for tracking individual activities and resources.
    • Simple & intuitive Text Editor: All web browsers and devices are supported by an editor that allows you to format text and add media and images.
    • Notifications: Users receive notifications of assignments, deadlines, forum posts, and private messages automatically.
    • Content Repository: Various external file repositories, such as Dropbox, Google Drive, and traditional computer uploads, can be used as repositories for a course.
    • User Roles: User roles are a collection of permissions assigned to different users across the system. Eg-Manager, Trainer, Non-Trainer, Content creator.
    • Live Engagement: Third-party integrations such as Zoom, Cisco WebEx, and Big Blue Button can structure live classes inside the course (additional licensing may be required).
    • Assessments: Multiple assessment options are available, such as rich, automated, and custom quizzes and assignments.
